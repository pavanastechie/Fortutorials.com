BITWISE OPERATORS

Bit wise operators are different from all other operators, these operators are not applicable to float, double.

Bitwise operators perform manipulate bits of integer. These operators are used for testing the bits, or shifting them to right or left.

Bitwise operators:

BITWISE AND (&)

BITWISE OR (|)

BITWISE XOR (^)

BITWISE NOT (~)

LEFT SHIFT (<<)

RIGHT SHIFT (>>)

UNSIGNED RIGHT SHIFT (>>>)
